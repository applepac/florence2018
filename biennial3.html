The proposal for the development of the Brazilian Pavilion at the 16th Venice Architecture Biennale 2018 is structured around a collective of four architects as curators of the exhibition themed Walls of Air.

The collection is curated by Gabriel Kozlowski, Laura González Fierro, Marcelo Maia Rosa and Sol Camacho.

Formed exclusively to conceive Brazils' contribution to the 16th Venice Architecture Biennale, the young architects' collective comes to represent the presence of Brazil in the world and the world in Brazil.

This choice reflects, from its conception, a first approach to the theme as it breaks with the country’s traditional practice of appointing a single curator, while reinforcing Fundação Bienal’s vision for its future in Venice: to build a more open, plural and participatory exhibit.
