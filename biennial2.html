Escola Sem Muros

School Without Walls

Escola Sem Muros is a program that aims to create a new model of teaching based on praxis and living social reality, and through this, build with the communities spaces of encounter, exchange, learning and local empowerment, empowering the actors involved.

Through a participative, educative and social methodology, we seek to unite the academic and empirical knowledge of the community itself to solve local problems, enabling students to create, design and build, applying their educational values as citizens in the transformation of the city.
